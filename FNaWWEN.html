Five Nights at Walter White is a comedic horror game made in Unity, based on the popular Five Nights at Freddy's franchise. We had to create it for a one-week GameJam, and I ended up making this project.

You play as Jesse Pinkman, who is trapped in the bathroom of Walter White's house, where Walter tries to get in at night. You need to cook enough meth to keep Walter White satisfied when he comes in, otherwise he will get angry and kill you. You can watch the cameras to see where Walter is at that moment, and you can call Saul Goodman once per night to send Walter back to the beginning.
